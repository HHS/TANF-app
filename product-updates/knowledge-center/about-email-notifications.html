About Email Notifications

Email notifications are sent out automatically to alert you when certain key actions in the TANF Data Portal (TDP). In this guide you can preview common email notifications that you may receive when interacting with TDP.

Not seeing email notifications?


We make every effort to ensure that the email notifications are delivered. If you do not see the email in your inbox, please check your "junk mail" or "spam" folder and add "no-reply@tanfdata.acf.hhs.gov" to your Safe Sender List.
