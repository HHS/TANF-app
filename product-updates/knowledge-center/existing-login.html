Use an Existing Login.gov Account

If you have an existing Login.gov account, follow these steps to get started with the TANF Data Portal (TDP).

  1. Sign into the TANF Data Portal

    Begin here: Visit the TANF Data Portal and select "Sign in with LOGIN.GOV for grantees"

  2. Sign In

    • Enter Email Address
    • Enter Password
    • Click 'Sign in'

  3. Request Access

    Request access to the TANF Data Portal.

    • Enter First Name
    • Enter Last Name
    • In alphabetical order, locate and select your Associated State, Tribe or Territory

  4. Request Submitted

    Your request for access is being reviewed by an OFA admin.

    You will receive a notification email from no-reply@tanfdata.acf.hhs.gov when your access request has been submitted. Please allow up to 1 business day for OFA admins to review your request.

  5. Approved for the TANF Data Portal

    Once your request for access has been reviewed and approved, you will receive another notification from no-reply@tanfdata.acf.hhs.gov and will be able to begin working in the TANF Data Portal.

    Note: We'll also notify you if your request is denied. Should this happen and you believe it to be in error please let us know by reaching out to TDP support at tanfdata@acf.hhs.gov.. Sign in to TDP to begin working in the TANF Data Portal.
